### Lesson 5: Overcoming Self-Limiting Beliefs

**Type:** Text

**Content:**
- **Text Content:**
```
Self-limiting beliefs are thoughts and assumptions that hold you back from reaching your full potential. They often sound like:
- "I'm not smart enough"
- "I don't deserve success"
- "I'm too old/young to start"
- "I'm not creative"
- "I always fail at this"

How Self-Limiting Beliefs Form:
- Past experiences and failures
- Messages from authority figures
- Comparison with others
- Fear of failure or rejection
- Cultural or societal expectations

Steps to Overcome Self-Limiting Beliefs:

1. Identify the Belief
   - Write down beliefs that hold you back
   - Notice when they appear in your thoughts

2. Challenge the Belief
   - Ask: Is this belief actually true?
   - Look for evidence that contradicts it
   - Consider alternative perspectives

3. Reframe the Belief
   - Replace limiting thoughts with empowering ones
   - Focus on growth and possibility
   - Use "yet" language (e.g., "I haven't mastered this yet")

4. Take Action
   - Start with small steps that challenge the belief
   - Build evidence of your capabilities
   - Celebrate progress, not just outcomes

Remember: Beliefs are not facts. You have the power to change them.
```
